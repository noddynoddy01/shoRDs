How to Upload a Paper

Share your research with the world in 3 simple steps

Step 1

🗂️ Open the Upload Tab

Tap the ✦ Upload button in the bottom navigation bar. This opens the paper submission screen.

Step 2

🔗 Provide the Paper Source

You have two options:

  • Paste a URL — arXiv link (e.g., arxiv.org/abs/...) or any DOI link
  • Upload a PDF — pick a file directly from your phone storage

Our AI parser reads the paper and extracts all metadata automatically.

Step 3

✅ Review & Publish

The AI fills in the title, summary, domain, tags, reading time, and key insights. Review them, make any edits, then tap Publish Brief. Your paper appears in the live feed instantly for everyone to discover.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is shoRDs free? ▾
Yes! shoRDs is free to download and gives you access to 5 research paper briefs. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited access, mentor chats, and multi-language support.
Can I upload my own research paper? ▾
Absolutely. Use the Upload tab to paste an arXiv/DOI link or upload a PDF. Our AI automatically extracts the metadata and creates a professional brief that others can discover.
Is it available on iOS? ▾
Currently shoRDs is only available on Android as an APK. An iOS version and Play Store listing are planned for the near future.
How does the AI Bot work? ▾
The AI Bot uses a built-in response engine that covers all app features and 15+ research domains. For smarter replies, connect your Gemini API key in Settings → AI Source → Gemini.
Can I become a mentor on shoRDs? ▾
Yes! Contact us using the form with your academic background and research focus. We'll onboard you to the mentor network where students can connect with you directly.
